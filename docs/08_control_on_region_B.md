On \(B=\{G>M\}\), the angular dissipation is active and the saturated algebra gives
uniform bounds like:
\[
\frac{G}{(1+G)^2}\le \frac{1}{1+M}.
\]

This yields a subcritical depletion estimate:
\[
\left|\int_B \frac{\omega^\top S\omega}{|\omega|^2+\varepsilon^2}\,dx\right|
\le \frac{c}{M}\,\mathcal D_{ang} + C_M\|\omega\|_2^2.
\]

Choose \(M\) large to absorb \(\frac{c}{M}\mathcal D_{ang}\) into dissipation.
